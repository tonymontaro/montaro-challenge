About

Become Unstoppable.

Software engineers/developers remain the most in-demand profession, and demand keeps increasing every year.

This project aims to encourage building problem-solving skills essential for success in software development. We intend achieve this through the fun activity of competitive programming and self-learning.

We also intend to build a network of skilled problem-solvers and connect such individuals with organizations looking for talent.

I was disqualified. Why?

We have zero-tolerance for cheating. If any form of cheating is detected, you will be disqualified from winning any prize. Subsequent offence by the same person might disqualify him/her from ever winning a prize.

I want to learn coding, where do I start?

There are numerous free tutorials for learning programming. We recommend Python as a first programming language for beginners. Here's a Free Tutorial for learning Python.

What is the schedule?

The Montaro Coding Competition hold on the last Saturday of every month. Prizes are usually distributed the next day, but might be delayed by up to a week.

When do I get my prize?

Prizes are usually distributed the next day after the competition, but might be delayed by up to a week.
